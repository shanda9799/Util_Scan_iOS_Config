Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Where are my scanned documents stored?
    All scanned documents are saved locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers unless you choose to share them.
  2. Do I need an account to use MobScan?
    No. MobScan works without any sign-up or login.
  3. What if the app crashes?
    We use Google Crashlytics to help identify and fix technical issues. Please update to the latest version to ensure you have the most stable experience.
  4. How can I share a scanned document?
    After scanning, tap the share icon and choose your preferred method (Email, Messages, AirDrop, etc.).
