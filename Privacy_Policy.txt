Privacy Policy for MobScan

Last Updated: 10/08/2025

MobScan (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is handled when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.
Our App does not require user registration or login.

The only data collected is:
	•	Anonymous crash and error reports to help us identify and fix bugs.
	•	This data may include:
	•	Device model and operating system version.
	•	App version.
	•	Time and type of crash or error.
	•	No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

⸻

2. How We Use the Data

The crash and error reports are used solely for:
	•	Identifying technical issues.
	•	Improving app stability and performance.

⸻

3. Data Storage & Security
	•	All scanned documents and images remain stored locally on your device.
	•	We do not automatically upload your files to any server.

⸻

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties, except:
	•	App bug/error reporting.
	•	When required by law.

⸻

5. Third-Party Services

Our App uses:
	•	Google Crashlytics for crash and error reporting.
You can read Google’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

⸻

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

⸻

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted within the App or on our website.
