FAQ

Does MobScan work offline? ▾

Yes. All scanning and enhancement run on‑device. You can export and share without internet.

How private is my data? ▾

We don’t collect your documents. Files are processed locally and stored on your device unless you choose to share or upload them.

Can I reorder or merge pages? ▾

Absolutely. Reorder by drag and drop, merge multiple scans, and export one clean PDF.

Is there a Pro version? ▾

The free version is powerful. Pro adds batch export, custom DPI/page size, and priority support.
